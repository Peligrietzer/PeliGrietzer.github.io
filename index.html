Peli Grietzer

I'm a researcher and writer specializing in ML, philosophy, and literary studies. I got my PhD from Harvard Comparative Literature in collaboration with the HUJI Einstein Institute of Mathematics, working with Stephanie Burt (literature), William Flesch (literature), and Tomer Schlank (math).

I'm currently working on the trade non-fiction adaptation of my 'mathematically informed philosophy of literature' research. I've given invited talks about this work in avenues including Cambridge University, University of Milan, UCLA, University of London, Hebrew University, Rensselaer Polytechnic, Johns Hopkins University, and University of Toronto.
